Boas práticas de **nomenclatura em APIs API-led** incluem o uso de nomes claros, descritivos e consistentes por camada.

#vr_en: |
**Naming best practices in API-led APIs** include using clear, descriptive, and consistent names per layer.  
Example: `customer-system-api`, `order-process-api`, `mobile-experience-api`.  
Avoid unclear acronyms and stick to the *<object>-<layer>-api* pattern.  
Use *kebab-case* for URLs and avoid language mixing.  
Standardization improves readability, maintenance, and team integration.

A **segurança no API-led** deve ser aplicada em todas as camadas:  
• Na camada *System*, use autenticação mútua e restrições de IP.  
• Em *Process APIs*, adote validação de tokens, rate limits e proteção contra ataques.

#vr_en: |
**Security in API-led** should be enforced across all layers:  
• In *System APIs*, use mutual authentication and IP restrictions.  
• In *Process APIs*, adopt token validation, rate limits, and attack protection.  
• In *Experience APIs*, implement CORS, OAuth 2.0, and input validation.  
Use **API Manager** to apply centralized policies and keep auditable logs.  
Security must be considered from design to deployment.

For *System APIs*, consider mutual authentication or certificates.  
Combine authentication with security policies in **API Manager** to strengthen access control.
